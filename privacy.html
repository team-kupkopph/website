Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information — in line with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

Last updated: . This policy is a starting template for our pre-launch website and will be expanded before the app launches. It is not legal advice — please have it reviewed by counsel.

1. Who we are

Kupkop PH (“we,” “us,” “our”) operates this website and the forthcoming Kupkop PH mobile app, an animal-welfare community for Filipino fur parents. You can reach us at team.kupkopph@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

On this pre-launch website, we only collect the email address you choose to give us when you join the waitlist or contact us. When the app launches, we will collect additional information needed to provide its features — for example, your name, account details, location data for rescue reports, and photos you upload. We will update this policy before that happens.

3. How we use your information

  • To notify you when the app launches and share related updates you’ve asked for.
  • To respond to your messages and requests.
  • To improve our website and understand interest in the project.

4. Legal basis & your consent

We process your information based on your consent, which you give by submitting the waitlist or contact forms. You may withdraw that consent at any time (see “Your rights” below).

5. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with service providers who help us operate (for example, an email or hosting provider), only as needed and under appropriate safeguards. We may also disclose information where required by law.

6. Data retention

We keep waitlist emails until the launch campaign is complete or until you ask us to remove you, whichever comes first.

7. Your rights under RA 10173

As a data subject, you have the right to be informed, to access, to object, to correct, to erase or block, to data portability, and to lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission. To exercise any of these, email us at team.kupkopph@gmail.com.

8. Security

We use reasonable organizational, physical, and technical measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

9. Children

This website is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors without appropriate consent.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the project develops. We’ll revise the “last updated” date above and, for significant changes, give notice where appropriate.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email team.kupkopph@gmail.com.
