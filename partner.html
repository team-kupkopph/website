Who we’re looking for

If you help animals, there’s a place for you

Big or small, formal or grassroots — if you care for animals in need, we’d love to have you on board.

Shelters & rescue orgs

Shelters and animal-welfare organizations caring for rescues and adoptables — registered or not. Both get the same tools; only the badge differs.

Pounds & LGU facilities

Local government pounds and facilities looking to rehome animals and coordinate volunteers.

Foster & rescue networks

Foster networks and independent rescuers who place animals into loving homes. A government ID and proof of billing are enough to get verified.

What you get

Reach, coordination, and direct support

Be discoverable

A verified profile that puts your animals and your cause in front of a community ready to adopt, donate, and volunteer near you.

Everything in one place

Manage rescues, adoption listings, inquiries, and Kawang-Gawa volunteering from a single dashboard — everything in one place.

Funds, direct to you

Abot-tulong donations go straight to your GCash or Maya. No middleman, no holding period, no cut.

What we ask

A simple, two-way commitment

Partnership works when the community can rely on you — and you can rely on us. Here’s the deal.

No fees. No exclusivity. No fund-holding. You stay fully in control of your animals, your listings, and your donations.

  • Keep your adoption listings reasonably current
  • Respond to adoption and volunteer inquiries in good time
  • Provide accurate information about your animals
  • Treat adopters, volunteers, and fellow rescuers with care
  • Complete verification so the community stays trusted & safe
Questions?

Partner FAQ

Is it free to join?

Yes — partnering is free during our launch. We’ll always be upfront before anything changes.

Do you take a cut of donations?

No. Abot-tulong donations go directly to your GCash or Maya account. Kupkop PH never holds or routes the money.

When does Kupkop PH launch?

We’re launching in Metro Manila first. Partners onboard ahead of time so you’re ready on day one.

What about our data and our animals’ information?

We handle data in line with the Data Privacy Act (RA 10173). See our Privacy Policy for details.

We’re a small, informal rescue group. Can we still join?

Absolutely. Foster networks and independent rescuers are welcome — the verification step just helps keep the community safe.

Ready to partner with us?

Tell us about your organization and we’ll be in touch to get you onboarded before launch.
