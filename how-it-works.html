How Sagip works

From spotted to safe

Sagip turns a moment of compassion into real action — connecting whoever sees an animal in need with the verified people who can help, then following the animal all the way to a home.

1

Report or spot

See a stray in need? Drop a pin on the map, add a photo and a few details. The nearest verified rescuers and shelters are alerted instantly — no phone tree, no guesswork.

2

Rescue & care

A verified rescuer claims the case so efforts aren’t duplicated. From pickup to vet to recovery, every status update is logged — and built-in chat keeps everyone coordinated.

3

Adopt or support

Once safe, the animal moves into adoption. Can’t adopt? Give through Abot-tulong, book a shelter walk, or share their story so the right home finds them faster.

More ways to help

Helping doesn’t stop at rescue

Sagip is a loop, not a one-off — there’s a way for everyone to take part.

Give via Abot-tulong

Donate to a shelter in seconds with GCash or Maya QR — your support goes straight to them.

Join a walk

Book a volunteer walk slot, sign the waiver, and give a shelter dog the day out they need.

Reunite lost pets

Report a lost or found pet and let smart matching surface likely reunions near you.
